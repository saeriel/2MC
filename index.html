ABOUT


bruise is project about fashion and sustainability. fashion is no longer defined by style, but by trends. consumers are trapped in an endless cycle of disposable, fast fashion; it's always out with the old, and in with the new.

so, bruise takes unwanted, 'unfashionable' clothing,
and gives them a new life.

art / design / styling : isabel fang

photography: zac yeow

model: dannick yong , hevina kaur
